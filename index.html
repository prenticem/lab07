About Us

Salmon Cookies was founded in 2020 with the goal of providing fresh, delicious cookies to the people of the Pacific Northwest. Our unique salmon-shaped cookies have become a local favorite, and we are proud to offer them at each of our locations. We use only the finest ingredients and have a commitment to sustainability in all aspects of our business. Come visit us today and taste the difference!
